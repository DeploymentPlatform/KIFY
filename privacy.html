Privacy Policy

How we collect, protect, and handle your data at Kify Hospital.

At Kify Hospital, we are dedicated to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of our patients' personal and medical information. This Privacy Policy details our practices concerning data collection, transmission, and security when utilizing our website services.

1. Data Collection and Usage

As a static educational and informative website, we do not deploy private databases or background tracking software that harvest personal data. The only situations in which you provide personal information are:

  • Static Consultation Request Forms: When you complete the appointment request form, the website temporarily reads the inputs (Name, Phone Number, Selected Department, Date, and Message) to construct a URL-encoded string.
  • WhatsApp Redirection: This data is compiled locally in your browser and redirected to the official Kify Hospital WhatsApp communication channel (+91 85000 23456) when you hit submit. We do not store this data on our servers.

2. Patient Confidentiality & Records

Kify Hospital adheres to strict medical ethics and Indian national healthcare standards. Your clinical consultation history, diagnostic tests, treatment records, and child growth assessments are strictly confidential. They are only shared with authorized medical staff directly involved in your care pathway.

3. Cookies and Analytics

We do not deploy invasive third-party tracking scripts, remarketing tags, or profiling systems. We use basic, privacy-preserving analytical cookies only to measure general visitor numbers and page popularity to improve website responsiveness.

4. Third-party Links

Our website features external links, including connections to Google Maps (for navigation directions) and Google Reviews. Please note that Kify Hospital is not responsible for the privacy practices or data policies of these external platforms.
