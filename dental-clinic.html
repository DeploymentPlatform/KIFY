Sterilization & Safety Protocols

Maintaining hygienic standards is our primary responsibility.

At Kify Dental Clinic, we implement layered sterilization processes to ensure patient health safety. This includes autoclave sterilization of metallic drills and hand instruments, fresh disposable gloves/cups/syringes for every single checkup, and chemical disinfection of dental chairs between appointments.

We perform daily biological tests on autoclave systems to ensure zero pathogen survival, protecting you and your children from infections.

Hygienic Checklist

  • ✓ Autoclaved Class-B instrument cycles
  • ✓ Strictly single-use dental disposables
  • ✓ Chemical aerosol chair wipe-downs
  • ✓ Daily sterilization biological audits
  • ✓ Certified medical waste disposal
